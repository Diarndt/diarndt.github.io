Montana Bicyle Adventure

Montana 6-Day Adventure

Enjoy the views that earned Montana its "Big Sky" nickname. Pedal through sacred native ground as you tackle the Continental Divide on Chief Joseph Pass. Soak up plenty of Lewis & Clark history, including the headwaters of the Missouri River. Ride the gorgeous Pioneer Mountains Scenic Byway through the ghost towns and abandoned mines of the Pioneer Mountain Range. Recharge in beautifully scenic lodges as you experience genuine Montana country along the eastern edge of the Rocky Mountain Range.

Moab Utah Sunrise Tour

Even though this tour leaves town at the crack of dawn, the magnificent views are worth waking up for! Join us for this memorable sunrise tour, complete with a delicious continental-style breakfast, unparalleled scenic vistas and a fun descent (3500 feet elevation loss!) down into Moab. This tour is perfect for those without a lot of mountain biking experience. That said, all riders need to be comfortable using hand brakes as well as be fit enough to pedal up some rising terrain.
